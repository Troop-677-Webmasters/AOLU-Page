What is Arrow of Light University (AOLU)?

Arrow of Light University (AOLU) is a premiere opportunity to earn multiple activity badges in a single weekend. The event is sponsored by the Scouts and adult leaders of Troop 677 as a service to busy Cub Scouting families.

Scouts will spend the day working on Arrow of Light requirements and also get to know the troop in a picturesque, nearby camp setting along the Missouri river.

Both boy and girl Webelos are welcome, but we request that adult female leadership camp with each girl den attending. Den leaders and parents are welcome also.

When & Where is AOLU 2021?

Troop 677 is hosting AOLU 2021 on November 6th-7th (Saturday-Sunday) at Klondike State Park, located in St Charles County (just 30 minutes from West St Louis County).


Costs

Cost is $5 for Webelos scouts to cover food and materials. Parents are welcome to attend free of charge. Cost includes program costs, meals, and camping (if desired) overnight. Scouts are not required to camp with the troop, but are welcome to do so. If camping, please bring your own equipment, although the troop can provide tents if needed. Paying for AOLU can be completed in several ways which are outlines in the registration section.
